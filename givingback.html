First Racquet

First Racquet, a youth-led nonprofit based in Houston, Texas, is committed to empowering underprivileged young athletes in Angola. Our mission is to nurture their passion for tennis and improve their lives. Your generous donations will be directed to these aspiring athletes, providing them the chance to swing their first racquet and discover their love for the sport. We believe tennis is more than a game—it’s a path to positive change, and together, we can make a significant difference.
