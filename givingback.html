First Racquet is a youth-led nonprofit based in Houston, Texas committed to empowering underprivileged young athletes in Angola. Thier mission is to nurture a passion for tennis and improve their lives. The organisation strongly believes that tennis is more than a game but a path to positive change, and together, we can make a significant difference.

The Malayalee Engineers’ Association of Houston, Texas is a professional, non profit, non political, and secular organization of engineering graduates of Malayalee origin. The organization provides a forum to professionally and socially interact, engage in charitable activities and find ways to promote organizational growth. Through its scholarship initiative, MEA provides financial assistance and mentoring to economically backward and academically brilliant undergraduate engineering students in India.

A vital nonprofit organization committed to distributing food and resources to those in need throughout the Houston community.
