Achievements

Celebrating milestones & stories of growth

November 2024

Volunteer Stringer – FirstRacquet.org

For over two years, I’ve been a dedicated volunteer stringer for FirstRacquet.org, even receiving a special thank you on their website (Thanks Note). Stringing racquets ensures players have access to top-notch equipment, maximizing their performance. This not only saves FirstRacquet valuable time and resources, but also strengthens our community.

It’s incredibly rewarding to connect with passionate players, dedicated coaches, and fellow volunteers, all united by a love for the sport. Together, we create a supportive and inspiring environment where young tennis players can truly thrive.

July 2023

Made Shadow Creek High School Varsity Tennis Team

I’m thrilled to share that I was selected for the Shadow Creek High School varsity tennis team for the 2024 season. It’s been a journey of hard work, perseverance, and passion for the sport.

Looking forward to representing my school and giving it my all on the court. Stay tuned for updates!

April 2022

Regional Officer – FirstRacquet.org

As a Regional Officer, I organized and coordinated tennis matches, managed event activities, oversaw scoring, handled donated equipment, and took part in racquet stringing as well as packaging/distribution. It’s been an honor to help the organization’s mission.

June 2022

District Champion – Tennis (A Singles)

Won the Tennis District Championship representing Nolan Ryan Middle School as the A-Singles player.

December 2020

Violinist – Junior High Varsity Group

Proud to have been part of the Nolan Ryan Junior High School varsity violin group.
