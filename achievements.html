Volunteer Stringer for FirstRacquet.org

Nov 2024

For over 2 years, I've been a dedicated volunteer stringer for FirstRacquet.org, even receiving a special thank you on their website. Stringing racquets ensures players have access to top-notch equipment, maximizing their performance. This not only saves FirstRacquet valuable time and resources, but also fosters a strong sense of community.
It's been incredibly rewarding to connect with passionate players, dedicated coaches, and fellow volunteers, all united by a love for the sport. Together, we create a supportive and inspiring environment where young tennis players can truly thrive.
