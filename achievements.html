Various Achievements

For over 2 years, I've been a dedicated volunteer stringer for firstracquet.org, even receiving a special thank you on their website (check it out here Thanks Note). Stringing racquets ensures players have access to top-notch equipment, maximizing their performance. This not only saves FirstRacquet valuable time and resources, but also fosters a strong sense of community. It's been incredibly rewarding to connect with passionate players, dedicated coaches, and fellow volunteers, all united by a love for the sport. Together, we create a supportive and inspiring environment where young tennis players can truly thrive.
