Achievements

Various Achievements

Nov 2024

For over 2 years, I've been a dedicated volunteer stringer for firstracquet.org, even receiving a special thank you on their website (check it out here Thanks Note). Stringing racquets ensures players have access to top-notch equipment, maximizing their performance. This not only saves FirstRacquet valuable time and resources, but also fosters a strong sense of community. It's been incredibly rewarding to connect with passionate players, dedicated coaches, and fellow volunteers, all united by a love for the sport. Together, we create a supportive and inspiring environment where young tennis players can truly thrive.

July 2023

I'm so excited to share some big news! I've officially made the Shadow Creek High School varsity tennis team for the 2024 season. It's been a long time coming, and I'm incredibly proud of myself for all the hard work and dedication I've put into the sport.

I can't wait to represent my school and give it my all on the court. Stay tuned for updates on the upcoming season!

April 2022

Proud to serve as the Regional Officer for FirstRacquet.org. My responsibilities include organizing and coordinating tennis matches, overseeing activities during events, maintaining scores, managing donated equipment, assisting with packaging and distribution, and stringing tennis racquets.

June 2022

Won the Tennis District Championship representing Nolan Ryan Middle School as the A Singles player.

December 2020

Proud to be playing violin for Nolan Ryan Junior High School varsity group.
