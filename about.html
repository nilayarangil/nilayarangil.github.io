About

Hi there! I'm Nilay Arangil, a passionate student at Shadow Creek High School in Pearland, Texas. My world revolves around four key areas: programming, coaching, volunteering, and tennis. This space serves as my hub to share upcoming courses, projects, and exciting volunteer opportunities within the Houston community.

Fueling Innovation with Code:

My fascination lies in crafting impactful applications through various programming languages. However, code takes on a whole new dimension with microprocessors like Raspberry Pi. These bridge the gap between the digital and physical world, allowing me to witness the practical impact of my code in real-time. Exploring large language models like ChatGPT and Bard further fuels my passion, opening doors to build innovative applications in natural language processing, code generation, and creative content creation.

From Student to Coach:

At the tender age of 9, I embarked on my coaching journey, sharing my knowledge of number sense with middle schoolers. Having myself earned first place in district competitions, I ignited a passion in others. Python classes followed at 10, enabling me to coach students from diverse backgrounds, ranging from young learners to engineering graduates. The guidance of my software engineer father played a crucial role in introducing me to the world of Raspberry Pi, sparking my exploration of its potential through various sensors.

Houstonmentors.com: A Community Initiative:

Driven by this passion, I launched "houstonmentors.com" in 2022, offering comprehensive courses in number sense, Python, and Raspberry Pi. Hosted on GitHub and powered by GoDaddy, this platform empowers individuals of all ages to explore the wonders of technology.

Beyond Technology:

While coding and coaching occupy a significant space in my life, other pursuits hold equal importance. Representing my school in singles tennis, I contributed to our district championship win in 2022. Several UTR tournament victories have fueled my determination to reach new heights in the sport. Music also holds a special place, with piano and violin being my instruments of choice. As part of the middle school orchestra, I enjoyed playing for the varsity team. In my free time, I express myself through sketching and painting.

Join the Journey:

Welcome to my space! Stay tuned for updates on upcoming courses, projects, and volunteering initiatives. Let's explore the exciting possibilities that technology, coaching, and community hold together!
